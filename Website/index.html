Fair Price

The easiest way to shop!

Vision Statement

Fair Price is an application that will be used for the basic purpose of buying and selling goods. The application allows all throughout the nation, to see products posted by sellers. Users as sellers will be able to post an advertisement of a product, accompanying the price and description, for that particular item.

Video

Intended Users

The application is designed for those wanting to buy or sell items which are listed on the platform, the item can be either used or a new one. Sellers can advertise their items, and connect with those who are interested in buying them. Buyers can browse the posts in their area (city), and purchase their item.

Why Use this App?

This Application makes it easy for people who have valuable items and want to get rid of it, to do so and those who need an item but dont want to pay the full retail price for it. The seller is making money, while the buyer is saving money. No matter what end of the app your on, Its always a win-win situation.

Postmortem

In Iteration 2, we worked mostly with our database which took most of our time to set up. Due to that we weren't able to complete as much features. We idendified that we have to handle hard issues together. Things went more smoothly in iteration 3 as we helped eachother more when we were stuck and completed features faster.

What took the most time? The least? Any surprises?

Setting up and using the database took the most of our time. The easiest and least time consuming was anything layout-related. There wasn't really any surprises.

How did the project change from your initial (iteration 0) vision or stories, or did it work out as predicted?

When we started iteration 0, we had a lot of big plans including the core of our app. We found out pretty fast that we would have to be more ideal and after that it was smooth sailing.
